About us

About 100 years ago, an inspired entrepreneur, Shri. Morrappasheth Bapusheth established
the first manufacturing and selling unit of Kaka Halwai at Budhwarpeth in Pune. Today the shop has
become a landmark of success with Kaka Halwai franchise spread all over the city.

Growth of Business

After Shri. Morrappasheth Bapusheth the endeavor was looked after by his sons, Shri.
Shankarrao M. Gadve and Mr.Somnathsheth Gadve. They garnered the business into becoming
the one-stop shop for sweets and namkeens. The key to their success was maintaining the
balance between tradition, taste, and variety.

Specialties

The quality and taste of our sweets and namkeen is without a doubt our biggest differentiator.
We have achieved it with our comprehensive infrastructure, skilled manpower, semiautomatic
manufacturing unit situated at easily accessible points. The hygienic handling and distribution of
fresh edibles is ensured by the strategic locations of our distribution hubs.

Vision

“To achieve the topmost position in the sweets and Namkeen industry by providing the best quality & fresh products to the customer while ensuring consumer satisfaction.”

Mission

“To make our customers healthier & happier by providing fresh, tasty and quality Sweets & Namkeen.”
